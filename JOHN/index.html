Our Vision

To create a world where every individual affected by cleft lip and palate receives the support, understanding, and care they deserve. We strive to raise awareness, foster community, and empower individuals through education, shared experiences, and opportunities for meaningful contributions, ensuring that no one faces the challenges of cleft alone.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and families affected by cleft conditions through education, support, and community engagement.
